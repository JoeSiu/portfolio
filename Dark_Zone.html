Dark Zone

A 2D top down shooter game made with C++ and SDL 2.0

Details

Dark Zone is a 2d top-down zombie shooter game. It is set in the near future, where a mutated virus had spread over the world and turned most humanity into zombies. Player have to complete a set of objectives and survive till the end. The game is made in C++ with SDL 2.0.
