Japan alley scene

A full CG time-lapse animation based on an alley in Kyoto

Details

A Japan old alley scene (an alley in Kyoto to be exact). Everything except the plants is modeled in Blender 2.83. Large or important objects are textured in substance painter (ground, wooden sign, etc), while others are just some basic PBR textures or blender's procedural texture.

The scene is available on both CGTrader and Gumroad:
CGTrader: https://www.cgtrader.com/3d-models/exterior/street/blender-japan-old-alley-scene
Gumroad: https://gum.co/bxECr

Creation date: June, 2020

Made in Blender 2.83
