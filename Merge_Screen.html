Merge Screen

A co-op puzzle game make in Unity. Showcased in SIG Playful Media 2019 at City University of Hong Kong

Details

Merge Screen is a co-op, first-person, split-screen puzzle game where the 2 players will have to "merge" two screens into a single level in order to solve the puzzles. The game is showcased to public in SIG Playful Media Showcase 2019 at City University of Hong Kong and got "CKJ Studio UI/UX Award" for best UI/UX design.

http://sweb.cityu.edu.hk/bsccm/sig2019/works/projectG08.html
https://www.facebook.com/324162731818496/posts/325402048361231/

Creation date: May, 2019
My role in this project: 3D modelling, Environment & Level design
