Path Of Ghost

 A 2D platformer puzzle-solving game submission for Global Game Jam 2021

Details

"LOSE your life and FIND your way" Path Of Ghost is a 2D platformer puzzle-solving game. Unlike a normal platformer, players will become a ghost after he dies in the game. Players need to use the ability of ghost such as floating, passing through walls and teleport from a stele to an altar, to get the keys and find the way to revive themselves, and get into the temple entrance.

Link: https://globalgamejam.org/2021/games/temp-4

Creation date: January, 2021
My role in this project: Level design, game system programming

Language used

Coding languages used on this project:
