Internship at 10Botics Limited

Summer internship as a 3D game designer at 10Botics Limited

Details

I had a summer internship as a 3D game designer at 10Botics Limited. 10Botics Limited is a company focusing on STEM education, and my role is to design and create 3D models, graphics and animation for game environment, participation in concept creation and innovation for various applications and to gather feedback from end-users and contribute to the product development cycle.

Donkey Car simulator

Donkey Car simulator is an open-source virtual sandbox simulator made in Unity3D and C# for playtesting the Donkey Car (a self-driving car). During the internship, I have added 3 new 3D environments and few more features (dynamic day/night cycle, random background & color for the track, car angle property used for training, etc.) which may help improve the training result for the car. For more details, I have also written a blog for the end result.

Donkey Car project

During the internship, I also have helped adding serval new features and make some improvements on their existing open-source Donkey Car projects using Python, which could facilitate their teaching for Donkey Car as well as making it easier to use together with the simulator. See Donkey Car project page for more details.
